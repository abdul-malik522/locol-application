Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ConnectLocal.

For Sellers

How do I list my products? expand_more

To list your products, navigate to the 'My Products' section in your seller dashboard and click 'Add New Product'. Fill in the details, upload high-quality photos, and set your price.

How do I manage my inventory? expand_more

You can update stock levels for each product in the 'My Products' section. We recommend updating your inventory regularly to avoid issues with orders.

For Restaurants

How can I find specific ingredients? expand_more

Use the search bar at the top of the home screen to look for specific ingredients. You can also browse by category or filter by location to find local sellers near you.

Orders & Payments

What payment methods are accepted? expand_more

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers through our secure payment gateway. All transactions are encrypted for your security.

How do I track my order? expand_more

Once your order is confirmed and out for delivery, you can track its status in real-time from the 'My Orders' section of the app. You'll also receive notifications at key stages.

Delivery

What are the delivery fees? expand_more

Delivery fees vary based on the distance between the seller and your restaurant. The fee will be calculated and displayed at checkout before you confirm your order.
